Big Trev’s Adventure!


Story:

You are playing as “Big Trev” a painter from southern Texas. Because of a violent incident with one of his co-workers, Big Trev was recently fired and is running out of money. As a last resort he has started looking for ancient treasure to pay off his debts. Help Big Trev find the treasure or he may go broke!



Controls:

Use the arrow keys, or wasd to move. You can also press escape to pause. Above the gameboard, there is a timer showing how many seconds you have left to beat the level. Touching an enemy or running out of time will result in a life lost. Once all three of your lives are gone, you lose!
